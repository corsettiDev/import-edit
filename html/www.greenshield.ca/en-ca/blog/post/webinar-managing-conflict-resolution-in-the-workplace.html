Webinar: Managing Conflict Resolution in the Workplace

Language: English/French 

Duration: 1 Hour 

Request more information about this webinar or related titles

This webinar aims to help leaders and their teams improve communication styles and patterns when managing workplace conflicts. The expert-led session will cover key communication strategies, including nurturing trust and respect when navigating open dialogue, common communication mistakes people make, tips for conflict resolution, and much more.
